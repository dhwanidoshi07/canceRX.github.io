About CanceRX

CanceRX is born out of partnerships with clinicians, care providers to improve outcomes and prognosis in cancer care, afflicted with sub-optimal prognosis and outcomes in general. We are driven by our mission to improve care at the point of delivery, and help patients at the grassroots level with access to best practices in cancer care. We empower health care providers with data driven technology to bridge the gap between diagnosis to treatment, thereby improving outcomes in cancer care.

Though our current focus is on oncology, our flagship product SUMRIZ and CDS can be used for other specialities as well, thereby helping patients with chronic conditions, globally

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there a free trial?

    We value what we do. Our solutions are minimally priced and we have a minimum of 10 trials at USD 15. For SUMRIZ,/SUMRIZ+CDS please snd us an email and we will initiate a payment request.

  • How can I use CanceRX?

    We are an easy-to-use solution. You can log in, using your email id, phone number, create a profile. Once we approve your profile, you can buy credits and start to use either SUMRIZ or CDS or both.

  • Can I use CDS and SUMRIZ separately or do I need to buy them together?

    You can use SUMRIZ, CDS separately, or both together. The pricing is different for both and the credits that you get will be based on what you shortlist, SUMRIZ, CDS, or SUMRIZ+CDS.

  • Can your solutions integrate with our Hospital Information Systems?

    Yes, we have APIs that help integrate with your existing workflows. Else your / hospital doctors can simply signup using your mobile number/email id, buy credits and start using our services. And we will integrate the data that you require into your existing workflows.

  • How do I pay for your solutions? What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all credit cards, international ones too. Else you can wire the monies using our account details. Please write to us to know more.

  • Will you save my credit card information?

    We do not save credit card information. If you click the save check box, then your information saves with the payment gateway provider.

  • How soon will my credits end?

    Your credits are linked to your usage. Before the end of your credit, we will give you a low credit alert warning.

  • Is there an auto-recharge facility?

    Yes, you can auto-recharge using your credit card.

  • Is there another payment option, if I don't want to use my credit card?

    Please ask us for our account details. We accept transfers from within and outside India.

  • If the hospital, where I am consulting, is paying for my subscription, can I use my personal email for login.

    Yes, if your email is in the list of subscribers, provided by your hospital

  • What will happen to my patient data if the hospital is paying for my subscription?

    The data remains with the payer. However, if your hospital permits, and gives a go-ahead in writing, we can share the details with you.

  • Can I change plans?

    Yes, you can change plans by sending us a request, provided you have the requisite credits in your account.

  • Can I cancel at my will?

    Yes, our subscriptions are per record +duration-based. Once you opt out, we will not recharge you.

  • Will my data be private and safe?

    Yes, we are a HIPAA compliant solution and follow best practices for security.

  • Can I as a Clinician, give suggestions on our own Clinical Guidelines?

    Yes, of course. And if published we even give you credits and accolades for the same.
